# CricketMind Series — Overview

**Series Title:** *Building CricketMind: An AI Product Manager's Journey Through the Model Layer*

**Substack tagline:** A 6-part series on fine-tuning NVIDIA's Nemotron for cricket domain adaptation — from model selection to production deployment. Written for PMs who want to understand the full AI value chain by building through it.

---

## Series Structure

| # | Title | Tone | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Why Every AI PM Should Fine-Tune a Model | Product | The case for hands-on model work, cricket as domain benchmark |
| 2 | Why Nemotron, Not Llama | Hybrid | Base model selection as a product decision, NVIDIA ecosystem |
| 3 | The Dataset Is the Product | Product | Data generation, distillation, quality gates — the 70% nobody sees |
| 4 | Fine-Tuning on RunPod — What Actually Breaks | Deep Tech | Real training, real errors, Nemotron-specific challenges |
| 5 | Evaluation Is Strategy, Not Metrics | Product | CricketBench design, LLM-as-judge, category weights as product thesis |
| 6 | From Weights to Product — Publishing and What's Next | Hybrid | HuggingFace, inference, the full value chain reflection |
